About Us

Who We Are

Green Gleab is a Nairobi-based environmental and sustainability consultancy focused on ESG strategy, ESIA, and delivering resilient development outcomes across the region.

Our Story

Founded in Nairobi, Green Gleab brings together interdisciplinary expertise in environmental science, social impact, GIS, and climate risk to help clients design and implement sustainable projects.

Our multidisciplinary team combines technical expertise in environmental science, social impact, GIS mapping, climate risk, and stakeholder engagement with a strong understanding of regulatory frameworks and international safeguards, including the World Bank Environmental and Social Standards (WBESS).
